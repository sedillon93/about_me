All About Shannon

Answer: Nope! Originally I am from a suburb outside of Philadelphia. I spent my first 18 years in the same town.

Question 2: In college I majored in chemistry

Answer: Not quite...I started out as a chemistry major but found that I loved geology more during my sophomore year and switched majors.

Question 3: I spent five years studying German in school.

Answer: Yep! My grandmother grew up in Germany and I started learning the language in middle school to connect more with her.

Question 4: I went to Colgate University in central New York.

Answer: Yep! I spent four years in beautiful rural New York. First time I experienced windchills up to -20s.

Question 5: Scuba diving is one of my favorite activities.

Answer: Yep! I got scuba certified for my senior thesis project researching coral reefs and loved it. If I could I would live in the reefs.
